# Caterpillar Climbing Game

## Pitch:
Do you believe that life is about the journey, the outcome, or something else... something more personal? In [Caterpillar Climbing Game], fuel by your natural instinct and skills you learn along the way, journey through life as an egg, larvae, and caterpillar., all with a single goal: to become a butterfly. Travel up the tallest tree around while interacting fellow caterpillars, avoiding threats, and grabbing a snack or two along the way (see that yummy looking leaf up there??). An introspective game letting you experience a daunting task with (many) little legs.

- Hatch from egg
- larvae to caterpillar 
- Climb up large tree (aspen?) -> eat leaves, talk to other caterpillars

- Goal is to reach the top
- Different final scene depending on...?
- Game ends when turn into butterfly, fly over environment
